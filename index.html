Why TakePlaces?

Wouldn't your journey be less stressful if you could see what your destination looks like at any given moment? Is the local coffee shop busy right now? Is there traffic on Old Street? Is there parking available? What's happening on Lester Square?

Using TakePlaces mobile app you can look at any street in real time. TakePlaces connects you to people who happen to be on the street you wish to go to and are willing to take a picture of it.

How does it work?

TakePlaces works in three simple steps:
1. Request a snapshot of a street you'd like to see.
2. Set the time for how long that request is relevant.
3. Receive the snapshot, thank the sender and close your request.

FAQ

What technology will TakePlaces be built on?
I am going to develop back end using Node.js and MongoDB. The app will be build with PhoneGap. The UI designer will create and code HTML5 front end.
When will TakePlaces be available for download?
The first beta is planned to be released in November 2013. It will be distributed among a small group of early adapters who will provide initial feedback for further improvements.
